Frequently asked

How much do agent services cost?

The first profile assessment is free. After that the work follows the industry standard: a percentage of the player's contract, or a flat fee for individual services (resume, video, market consultation). Terms are discussed after the assessment.

Do you guarantee a contract?

No. Guaranteed contracts do not exist — the club always decides. What we guarantee is an honest level assessment, a quality presentation and targeted work with clubs your profile fits.

How long does it take to find a club?

The main window is April to July, when clubs build their rosters. Targeted openings also appear during the season. The real timeline depends on level, position, passport and how flexible you are on leagues.

Which countries do you work with?

The North (Sweden, Finland, Norway, Denmark), the Centre (Germany, Czechia, Slovakia, Austria), the West (Switzerland, France, the Netherlands) and other European markets — the specific countries depend on the player's profile.

What do I need to send for an assessment?

Your Elite Prospects link, stats from recent seasons, video (highlights and/or a full game), year of birth, position, citizenship and contract status.
