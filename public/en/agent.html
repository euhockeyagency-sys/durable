How the work is built

From the first assessment
to the transfer

Working with a player starts with an honest assessment: a realistic league level, the strengths and weaknesses of the profile, and the countries and divisions that fit. Then comes preparing the presentation — a hockey resume and video — and reaching out directly to clubs the player genuinely fits by position, passport and budget. The results of that work are on the confirmed transfers page, and the full list of services is in the Services section. Not sure where to aim — try the level calculator.
