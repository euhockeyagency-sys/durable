    How it's calculated

    No "magic"
    and no inflated promises

    The main factor is the level of the league you play in now: that's what clubs compare players by first. Then come production relative to position, age (under 20 they look at potential, after 25 at a finished level) and passport: EU citizenship doesn't make a player better, but it removes import limits and widens the list of clubs. The full reference of divisions is in the European leagues database.
