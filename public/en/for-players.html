What to prepare

A complete package lets us assess you faster and more accurately.

  1. Elite Prospects An up-to-date profile with stats
  2. Video Highlights and at least one full game
  3. Details Year of birth, position, height, weight, shot

Selection

What we assess before representation

A strong season alone is not enough. We evaluate whether the full profile fits a real club market.

Required

  • Accurate identity, age and citizenship data
  • Current stats and Elite Prospects
  • Recent full-game video
  • Clear contract status and availability
  • A realistic career objective

Reasons to pause or decline

  • Unverifiable or inconsistent information
  • No current game footage
  • A target level far above the demonstrated level
  • No suitable market for the position and passport
  • Unclear contractual obligations

Citizenship affects registration and import status; it is not used as a substitute for sporting assessment.

Frequently asked

Can you guarantee a contract?

No. The club always decides. An agency can assess your profile, prepare your materials, find the right markets and present you.

How long does it take to find a club?

It depends on level, position, passport, each team's recruiting calendar and the quality of your materials. There is no universal timeline.

Do you work with North American players?

Yes — with players from the USA and Canada, including those coming out of junior, USHL, NCAA or ACHA who want to keep playing in Europe. We know the import limits and the paperwork involved.

I'm 16-18. Can I move to Europe?

Yes, there are junior leagues and academies, but a move only makes sense into a program that gives you ice time and development. We assess each case individually — a junior move does not forgive mistakes.

I don't have an EU passport. Is that a problem?

It's a limitation, not a dead end. Most leagues have import spots — they go to players who clearly strengthen the roster. The assessment has to account for this: where your profile clears an import spot and a work visa, and where it doesn't.
