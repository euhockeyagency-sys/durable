How to read the table

Level matters more
than the country name

The second division of a strong hockey country is often higher in level than the top division of a small one. So the choice isn't a "dream country" but the level your profile matches. Not sure where to aim — try the level calculator: it suggests a realistic band of divisions in a minute.
