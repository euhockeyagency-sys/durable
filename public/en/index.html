Frequently asked

How much does a hockey agent cost?

The first profile assessment is free. After that the work follows the industry standard: a percentage of the contract, or a flat fee for individual services. More on the services page and the about the agent page.

Do you guarantee a contract?

No. The club always decides. We assess your level honestly, build a strong presentation and present you directly to clubs that fit — without promises that are not ours to make.

Which countries do you work with?

Sweden, Finland, Norway, Denmark, Germany, Czechia, Slovakia, Austria, Switzerland, France, the Netherlands and other European markets — the specific countries depend on your level, passport and age.

What do I need to send for an assessment?

Your Elite Prospects link, stats, video, year of birth, position, citizenship and contract status. Send your details on the contact page.

How do I know what level I can realistically reach?

It depends on your age, stats, position and passport. We give you an honest band of countries and divisions — try the level calculator for a realistic estimate in a minute.
