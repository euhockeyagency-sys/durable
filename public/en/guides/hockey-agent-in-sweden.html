Hockey agent in Sweden: player assessment and club search

A practical explanation of work around the Swedish market. EHA does not claim an office in Sweden or guarantee contracts; the task is to assess the profile realistically and present it to suitable recipients.

What makes the Swedish market different

Sweden has a strong senior and junior competition pathway. Its reputation also creates intense competition: wanting to play in Sweden is not enough. Clubs assess pace, decision-making, play away from the puck, current league, age and readiness for a defined role.

Levels under consideration

SHL, HockeyAllsvenskan and HockeyEttan differ in budget, pace and profile requirements. For a junior, age eligibility, expected ice time, education and accommodation also matter. See the Hockey in Sweden country guide for league structure and official sources.

Work before outreach

  1. match the player to a realistic level;
  2. check statistics, video and Elite Prospects;
  3. confirm citizenship, contract and playing rights;
  4. define a role that can be presented clearly;
  5. select relevant clubs instead of mass emailing.

Why the passport matters but is not decisive

Citizenship affects work rights, registration and import status. Administrative convenience cannot compensate for a sporting mismatch. For a professional athlete from outside the EU/EEA, requirements must be checked against Swedish authority guidance and the individual contract.

The player package

  • current statistics and team role;
  • an accurate Elite Prospects profile;
  • short highlights and a full game;
  • position, age, measurements and shot;
  • passport, contract status and availability;
  • realistic level and contract expectations.

Possible assessment outcomes

An honest assessment may lead to outreach, but it may also recommend a different target league, updated video or another season proving the level elsewhere. That is more useful than promising Sweden when the current profile has no verified market.

FAQ

Can a club in Sweden be guaranteed?

No. The club decides, and availability depends on its roster and the season.

Are non-EU players considered?

The possibility depends on level, role and immigration route and must be checked for the specific club.

Are highlights or a full game more important?

Both are useful: highlights give a quick overview, while a full game shows decisions and consistency.
